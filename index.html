Working theory
Core question: can U.S./Israeli strikes suppress meaningful missile and drone launch capability before political and economic costs force them to back off? If yes, the regime becomes much more vulnerable. This dashboard tests that theory with daily tempo, target spread, oil stress, and business risk — not cumulative scoreboard theater.
